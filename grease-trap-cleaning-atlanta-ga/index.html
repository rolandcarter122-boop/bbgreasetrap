Frequently Asked Questions

How often should a grease trap be cleaned?

Most commercial kitchens in USA require cleaning every 1-3 months based on volume and local code.^^

What is the difference between a grease trap and a grease interceptor?

A grease trap is a smaller unit used indoors; a grease interceptor is a larger outdoor or underground system for high-volume flows.

Can I dispose of cooking oil directly down the drain?

No. Pouring large volumes of oil down the drain overloads the system and is usually prohibited by local regulations in USA.

What happens if my grease trap overflows or backs up?

You risk plumbing backups, foul odors, health code penalties and expensive cleanup.

Do you provide installation services for traps and interceptors in USA?

Yes, we design, install and test new systems to meet local requirements and site usage.
